4. **Continue**: Ask follow-up questions anytime

## üí° Tips

- **Be specific**: Include phase names, item names, or project names
- **Natural language**: Ask questions as you would to a colleague
- **Keywords work**: "Phase 1", "cement", "vendor", "compare"
- **Dates optional**: Say "this month" for recent purchases
- **Case insensitive**: "PHASE 1" and "phase 1" both work
